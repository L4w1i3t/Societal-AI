Our Foundation

Shaping Tomorrow's AI Landscape

We bridge the gap between technological advancement and humanistic values, creating a future where AI serves all of humanity.

"The true potential of AI lies in its ability to uplift humanity while safeguarding and empowering future generations."
— Amit Ray

Our Purpose

We aim to raise awareness that AI technology will significantly reshape our lives. The AI 2027 Project serves as a wake-up call: by 2027, powerful agentic AI systems may become deeply integrated into society. We work toward building an awesome AI community that can be adopted everywhere.

Our Values

The future of AI belongs to everyone, not just tech people. We embrace interdisciplinary thinking, bringing together diverse voices from humanities and technology. No matter your background—computer science or otherwise—you are welcome here.

Our Mission

Our mission is to engage UVA students and faculty in meaningful dialogues about AI's impact. By 2027, we envision a UVA where students and faculty are equipped, informed, and empowered to confront the era of agentic AI.
